Our Origin Story

From shared frustrations to building a different kind of engineering firm

In 2024, Sam Kilonzo (CEO) and Daniel Muumbi (CTO) founded TechAlchemist Solutions with a singular mission: to bring real engineering discipline to software development. After years at Fortune 500 tech companies witnessing the same patterns of technical debt accumulation and short-term thinking, they decided to build an alternative.

The name "TechAlchemist" reflects our philosophy - we combine the art of software craftsmanship with the science of engineering principles. Unlike traditional alchemists who sought to turn lead into gold, we transform complex business problems into elegant, scalable solutions through applied computer science.

What began as two engineers working on challenging projects has grown into a respected engineering practice, but we've maintained our founding commitment to technical excellence over growth for growth's sake. Every system we architect bears the hallmark of our engineering-first approach.

Our Engineering Principles

The foundational beliefs that guide every system we design and build

🧮

Mathematical Rigor

We apply formal methods and computer science fundamentals to create systems with provable properties, not just hopeful implementations.

⚖️

Tradeoff Awareness

Every architectural decision involves tradeoffs. We make these explicit and document them thoroughly for future maintainers.

🛡️

Failure Anticipation

Systems fail in unexpected ways. We design for failure modes first, success paths second.

📈

Measurable Quality

Quality attributes must be quantified - from performance metrics to reliability SLOs. If you can't measure it, you can't improve it.

🧪

Empirical Validation

Architectural decisions must be validated through prototyping and measurement, not just theoretical analysis.

📚

Knowledge Sharing

We document not just what we built, but why we built it that way - ensuring long-term maintainability.
